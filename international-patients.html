For Patients from Abroad

Specialist ENT care, made simple when you're far from home.

Hyderabad sees patients from across the Gulf, East Africa, South Asia, and beyond — drawn by surgical expertise, modern facilities, and treatment costs that make a meaningful difference. Dr. Naseer's ENT has cared for international patients for over two decades. Here is what to expect.

Why patients travel here.

Our international patients return — and refer their families — for a consistent set of reasons. Quality of care, transparent pricing, English-fluent consultation, and a clinic that respects your time.

Surgical expertise

Endoscopic sinus and ear surgery, cochlear implants, microlaryngeal work — performed in-house by an experienced consultant.

Transparent pricing

Written all-inclusive estimates before you travel. No surprises on arrival; no upselling on the day of surgery.

Multilingual care

Consultations available in English, Urdu, Hindi, Telugu, and basic Arabic — your treatment plan, clearly explained.

Same-day workup

Audiometry, endoscopy, and most decisions completed in a single visit — important when you have limited days in country.

What we commonly treat for visitors.

The conditions most often brought to us by patients flying in. If you don't see your concern here, send us a WhatsApp — most ENT presentations are well-suited to a focused stay in Hyderabad.

Chronic ear disease

Eardrum perforations, cholesteatoma, otosclerosis — including myringoplasty, mastoidectomy, and stapes surgery.

Sinus disease & allergy

Long-standing sinusitis, nasal polyps, deviated septum — FESS, balloon sinuplasty, septoplasty.

Pediatric ENT

Adenoids, recurrent tonsillitis, glue ear with grommets — single-anaesthetic combined procedures in selected cases.

Voice & airway

Vocal-cord lesions, hoarseness, snoring and obstructive sleep apnea — including microlaryngeal surgery.

Hearing & balance

Audiometric workup, hearing-aid fitting, vestibular assessment, and cochlear implantation candidacy.

Endoscopic workup

Diagnostic nasendoscopy and laryngoscopy — many puzzles resolved in a single in-clinic examination.

How a typical visit is arranged.

From first message to follow-up at home — the process is built around your time. We try to compress decisions, scans, and treatment into the shortest stay that delivers safe outcomes.

WhatsApp or email first contact

Send us your symptom summary, age, any prior reports or scans, and your preferred travel window. We respond within 24 hours with an initial opinion and an outline of what an in-person assessment will involve.

Video pre-consultation (optional)

For complex cases we offer a 15–20 minute video call before travel. You meet the doctor, review existing imaging, and agree on the scope of the visit.

Visa invitation & estimate

We can issue a medical-visa invitation letter for the patient and one attendant, and share a written cost estimate covering consultation, investigations, surgery, hospitalisation, and a typical post-op stay.

Arrival & assessment

On your first day in Hyderabad: detailed consultation, examination, audiometry / endoscopy / imaging as required, and a clear plan with timelines. We block time so you are not waiting long.

Treatment & observation

Outpatient procedures the same week. Day-care or admitted surgeries scheduled within 2–4 days of arrival in most cases. You stay locally for the required observation period only — typically 3–7 days for ear / nose / throat surgery.

Discharge & fly-home clearance

Before you fly back, we provide a written summary, medication list, post-op care instructions, and a fitness-to-travel letter where required.

Remote follow-up

Structured WhatsApp / video reviews at the agreed intervals (typically week 1, 4, and 12). We co-ordinate with your home doctor for any local care needed — sutures, audiogram, wound check.

What to bring with you.

The more we know on day one, the less needs to be repeated. A short, organised pack of records saves you both time and money.

  • Passport, visa, and a copy of the medical-visa invitation letter we provide
  • All previous ENT records — clinical notes, prescriptions, treatment summaries
  • Audiograms, tympanograms, and any speech-audiometry reports
  • CT or MRI scans on disc/DVD or as DICOM files — not just the printed report
  • Surgical reports from any prior ENT or related operations
  • A current medication list, including any blood thinners and allergies
  • Recent blood work and ECG if you are over 50 or have known cardiac issues
  • Contact details of your home doctor for post-treatment coordination

Consultations in English, Urdu, Hindi, Telugu, and basic Arabic. We are happy to arrange a translator for other languages on request.

Airport & stay

Rajiv Gandhi International (HYD) is roughly 35–45 minutes from the clinic. We can suggest hotels and serviced apartments within a 10-minute drive — affordable, clean, and family-friendly.

Payment & insurance

We accept Indian rupees in cash, UPI, debit/credit card, and standard international wire transfer. We help with documentation for insurance reimbursement in your home country.

Privacy & records

All medical records are handled per our Privacy Policy. We provide copies on request and can share to your home doctor with your written consent.

Clinic hours

Outpatient consultations are 6 PM – 9 PM, Monday to Saturday. Surgical scheduling is morning. We can hold a slot for incoming international patients.
